About Sanyukt Karmyogi Sena

Who We Are

Sanyukt Karmyogi Sena, which translates to "United Army of Dutiful Workers," is a non-governmental organization founded on the principles of selfless service and community collaboration. We are a group of dedicated individuals from diverse backgrounds united by a common goal: to bring about positive and sustainable change in society.

Our Vision

We envision a society where every individual has the opportunity to live a dignified life, with access to basic necessities like education, healthcare, and a clean environment. We strive to create a network of volunteers who are not just donors, but active participants in the process of community building.

What We Do

Our work is focused on tangible, on-ground projects. We organize cleanliness drives, tree plantation events, educational workshops for underprivileged children, health check-up camps, and distribution drives for essential supplies. We believe that small, consistent actions by many can lead to large-scale transformation.
